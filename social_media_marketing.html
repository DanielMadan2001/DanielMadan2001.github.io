Social Media & Marketing

In addition to programming and game design, I have continuously improved my abilities in social media management and marketing. I have held several marketing positions that involved creating public updates, statements, and graphics on behalf of large groups. In my free time, I enjoy sharing memes on personal and comedic accounts.

Due to dissatisfaction with the corporate decisions made by Facebook and X, I have stopped posting to my personal accounts. At present, I only engage with social media through corporate and comedic accounts.

World Community Grid

During my time as a communications intern at World Community Grid, I was engaged in multiple aspects of the company's marketing process. As the company was undergoing a transitionary period, I would frequently post updates about the state of the system to their X and Facebook pages, as well as their website's public forum. When concerned users sent emails to the group's email, I would answer their questions in a polite and informative manner.

I co-wrote dozens of articles featuring news about the state of the platform and research updates from partner projects. I was fortunate enough to share the stories of two schools that incorporated grid computing into their curriculum.
