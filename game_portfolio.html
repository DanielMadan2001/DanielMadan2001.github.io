Game History

Proto Man

A Mega Man fan-game project starring the character Proto Man. The project has been abandoned, but an editable version is available with functional platforming control.

Queenslayer

A 2D fighting game with fast paced combat, free-form combos and competitive multiplayer. Features controller and keyboard support for PC players. Currently in development.

Scrabbleman

A word puzzle game that combines elements of Hangman and Srabble. The objective is to solve a series of word-guessing puzzles while strategically choosing uncommon letters. Players are awarded points based on their speed and ability to select low-value letters.

Sellsword Company

A resource management game created in Python. The player takes the role of a mercenary guild leader who assigns units to tasks according to their strengths and weaknesses.
