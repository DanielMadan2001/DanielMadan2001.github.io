School Projects

Stellar Synergy

Stellar Synergy has been a collaborative project between Osamudiamen Adun, Stefan D'Ippolito, Rebecca De Venezia, Jayden Ting and myself for the class CISC 496.

The game is a collaborative 2D platformer that uniquely tests players' communicative and teamwork abilities. Teams of two players control the same mech as they traverse hostile alien landscapes filled with tricky terrain and enemies. Actions that the mech can perform are split between both players, making even the most basic actions difficult with poor communication. Additionally, the game is controlled using the players' phones by connecting to a localized server. For more details, click here.

The demo version of the game has received universal praise and positive feedback from our teacher and peers. At the school's annual Creative Computing Showcase, the project was given the Best Videogame Award from the department's faculty.

Current Personal Projects

Queenslayer

A 2D fighting game emphasizing fast, arcadey gameplay and unique RPG system mechanics such as item upgrades, varied stat allotments, and mana/stamina meters. Created by myself and Matthew Krpac.

The game is in active development, but an alpha demo version is available for download on the official website.

Completed Personal Projects

Scrabbleman

A word puzzle game that combines elements of Hangman and Scrabble. The objective is to solve a series of word-guessing puzzles while strategically choosing uncommon letters. Players are awarded points based on their speed and ability to select low-value letters.

Sellsword Company

A resource management game created in Python. The player takes the role of a mercenary guild leader who assigns units to tasks according to their strengths and weaknesses.
